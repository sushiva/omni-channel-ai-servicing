## Overview

This policy covers all aspects of credit and debit card services including activation, replacement, upgrades, controls, and troubleshooting. Our goal is to provide secure, convenient card services that meet customer needs.

## Card Activation

### New Card Activation

**Activation Methods:**
1. **Mobile App** (Recommended)
   - Open app → Card Services → Activate Card
   - Scan card or enter 16-digit number
   - Confirm last 4 digits of SSN
   - Instant activation confirmation

2. **Online Banking**
   - Login → Account Services → Activate New Card
   - Enter card details and security code
   - Set PIN if debit card
   - Confirmation email sent

3. **Phone Activation**
   - Call 1-800-555-ACTIVATE (228-4828)
   - Available 24/7
   - Follow automated prompts
   - Have card and ID ready

4. **ATM Activation** (Debit Cards Only)
   - Insert card at any bank ATM
   - Enter temporary PIN (mailed separately)
   - Change to permanent PIN
   - Make a balance inquiry to complete

**Activation Timeline:**
- Must activate within 45 days of receiving card
- Inactive cards automatically cancelled after 45 days
- New card ships within 3-5 business days after cancellation request

**What Happens After Activation:**
- Old card immediately deactivated
- Can use new card immediately
- Recurring payments may need updating
- Temporary PIN changes to permanent PIN (debit only)

### Replacement Card Activation

**After Reporting Lost/Stolen:**
- Old card cancelled immediately
- New card number issued
- Activate using same methods as new card
- Update stored card info with merchants

**After Card Expiration:**
- Replacement card mailed 4-6 weeks before expiration
- Same card number (usually)
- Updated expiration date and CVV
- Activate upon receipt
- Old card stops working on expiration date

## Card Replacement

### Reasons for Replacement

**Lost or Stolen Card:**
- **Immediate Action**: Call 1-800-555-FRAUD (37283) immediately 24/7
- Card deactivated to prevent unauthorized use
- Zero liability for fraudulent charges if reported promptly
- Emergency card available at branch
- Standard replacement: 3-5 business days
- Expedited shipping: 1-2 business days ($15 fee)

**Damaged or Malfunctioning Card:**
- Chip not reading properly
- Magnetic stripe worn
- Card warped or cracked
- Name/number illegible
- Free replacement for defective cards

**Compromised Card:**
- Merchant data breach notification
- Suspected skimming device exposure
- Proactive replacement by bank
- Zero liability protection
- Expedited shipping at no cost

**Expired Card:**
- Automatic replacement sent 4-6 weeks before expiration
- No action needed from customer
- Activate new card upon receipt
- Old card deactivated on expiration date

### Replacement Process

**Step 1: Report**
- Call customer service: 1-800-555-BANK (2265)
- Report through mobile app or online banking
- Visit branch in person
- State reason for replacement

**Step 2: Verification**
- Verify identity with security questions
- Confirm mailing address
- Review recent transactions for fraud
- File police report if theft (recommended)

**Step 3: Card Issued**
- New card number generated (lost/stolen)
- Same number reissued (damaged/expiration)
- Shipped to address on file
- Tracking number provided for expedited shipping

**Step 4: Activation**
- Activate using methods listed above
- Set new PIN for debit cards
- Update recurring payments
- Destroy old card securely

**Replacement Fees:**
| Reason | Fee |
|--------|-----|
| Lost/Stolen | $5 (waived first time per year) |
| Damaged/Defective | Free |
| Expiration | Free |
| Design Upgrade | Free (once per year) |
| Expedited Shipping | $15 |
| International Replacement | $50 |

## Card Types and Features

### Debit Cards

**Features:**
- Direct access to checking account funds
- PIN or signature transactions
- ATM access worldwide
- Overdraft protection available
- Daily spending limits: $3,000 purchases, $500 ATM

**Rewards Debit:**
- 1% cash back on signature purchases
- No annual fee
- Rewards deposited monthly
- Eligible purchases only (excludes ATM, PIN)

### Credit Cards

**Standard Credit Card:**
- Variable APR based on creditworthiness
- No annual fee
- 1% cash back on all purchases
- $5,000-$25,000 credit line

**Premium Credit Card:**
- Lower APR for qualified customers
- $95 annual fee (waived first year)
- 2% cash back on gas and groceries, 1% on everything else
- Travel insurance and rental car coverage
- Extended warranty protection

**Secured Credit Card:**
- For building or rebuilding credit
- Cash deposit required ($300-$5,000)
- Deposit amount equals credit line
- Graduate to unsecured after 12 months of on-time payments
- No annual fee

### Specialty Cards

**Student Credit Card:**
- Lower credit limits ($500-$2,500)
- No credit history required
- Financial education resources
- No annual fee
- Rewards for good grades

**Business Credit Cards:**
- Higher credit limits
- Expense tracking tools
- Employee card issuance
- Annual fee varies by tier
- Enhanced rewards on business categories

## PIN Management

### Setting/Changing PIN

**Debit Card PIN:**
- Set during activation
- Change at any ATM
- Change via phone: 1-800-555-PIN (746)
- 4 digits required
- Cannot use sequential (1234) or repeated (1111) numbers

**Credit Card PIN (for Cash Advances):**
- Mailed separately with card
- Change same methods as debit
- Rarely needed (most transactions signature-based)

**PIN Security:**
- Never share your PIN
- Cover keypad when entering
- Don't write PIN on card
- Change if compromised
- Different PIN for each card recommended

**Forgotten PIN:**
- Reset online or via mobile app
- Call customer service for immediate reset
- Visit branch with valid ID
- New PIN effective immediately

**Locked PIN:**
- 3 incorrect attempts locks card
- Call customer service to unlock: 1-800-555-BANK (2265)
- May need to answer security questions
- Can reset PIN during unlock process

## Card Controls and Security

### Spending Controls (via Mobile App or Online)

**Transaction Limits:**
- Set daily spending maximum
- Per-transaction amount limit
- Number of transactions per day
- Reset limits at midnight

**Geographic Controls:**
- Restrict to domestic transactions only
- Allow/block international use
- Country-specific permissions
- State/region restrictions

**Merchant Category Controls:**
- Block specific merchant types:
  - Gas stations
  - Restaurants
  - Online merchants
  - ATMs
  - Gambling establishments
- Allow only approved categories

**Card On/Off:**
- Instantly disable card
- Re-enable anytime
- Doesn't affect recurring payments already authorized
- Useful when card temporarily misplaced

### Travel Notifications

**Why Notify Us:**
- Prevents card being blocked for suspicious activity
- Ensures card works while traveling
- Reduces fraud alerts interrupting your trip

**How to Notify:**
- Mobile app: Card Services → Travel Plans
- Online banking: Security → Travel Notification
- Call customer service: 1-800-555-BANK (2265)
- Visit branch before departure

**Information Needed:**
- Destination(s)
- Travel dates
- Which card(s) you're taking
- Contact number while traveling

**International Travel Tips:**
- Notify bank 3-5 days before departure
- Bring backup card from different card network
- Know your international withdrawal fees
- Have bank's international collect number
- Consider travel credit card for better rewards

### Fraud Monitoring

**Real-Time Alerts:**
- SMS/email for transactions over threshold
- Unusual merchant category alerts
- Foreign transaction notifications
- Large purchase confirmations
- Multiple declined transactions

**AI Fraud Detection:**
- Analyzes your spending patterns
- Flags unusual activity
- May temporarily block card
- You confirm/deny via text response
- Card reactivated once confirmed legitimate

## Card Benefits and Protections

### Purchase Protection

**Extended Warranty:**
- Doubles manufacturer warranty up to 1 year
- Covers mechanical failure
- Must pay with eligible credit card
- File claim within 90 days of failure
- Up to $10,000 per claim

**Purchase Security:**
- Covers theft or damage within 90 days of purchase
- Up to $500 per claim, $50,000 per year
- Deductible: $25
- Original receipt required
- Claim must be filed within 30 days of incident

**Return Protection:**
- If merchant won't accept return
- Up to 90 days from purchase
- $300 per item, $1,000 per year
- Must attempt return to merchant first
- Not for consumables or custom items

### Travel Benefits

**Travel Accident Insurance:**
- Up to $500,000 coverage
- When common carrier fare charged to card
- Covers accidental death or dismemberment
- 24/7 travel emergency assistance

**Rental Car Insurance:**
- Primary coverage for collision/theft damage
- Decline rental company coverage to activate
- Up to actual cash value of vehicle
- Coverage for up to 31 consecutive days

**Trip Cancellation/Interruption:**
- Reimburses non-refundable travel expenses
- Up to $1,500 per trip
- Must charge entire trip to card
- Covered reasons: illness, severe weather, jury duty

**Lost Luggage Reimbursement:**
- If airline loses luggage permanently
- Up to $3,000 per passenger
- Must file claim with airline first
- Bank coverage is secondary

### Zero Liability Protection

**Coverage:**
- $0 liability for unauthorized transactions
- Credit and debit cards covered
- Must report promptly (within 60 days)
- Cannot be negligent (e.g., gave PIN away)

**Reporting:**
- Immediate: Call 1-800-555-FRAUD (37283)
- Online: Report through mobile app or online banking
- Review statements monthly for unauthorized charges
- Report missing card immediately even if no charges yet

## Card Upgrades and Downgrades

### Upgrading Your Card

**Product Changes:**
- Standard to premium credit card
- Regular debit to rewards debit
- Secured to unsecured credit card
- Usually keeps same account number

**Eligibility:**
- Good payment history
- Meet credit score requirements
- Sufficient income
- Low debt-to-income ratio

**Process:**
- Request via online banking, phone, or branch
- Credit review may be required
- Approval within 1-2 business days
- New card mailed within 7-10 days
- Annual fee may apply

### Downgrading Your Card

**Reasons to Downgrade:**
- Avoid annual fee
- Simplify card features
- Lower credit line (control spending)

**Process:**
- Request downgrade before annual fee posts
- Usually approved immediately
- May keep same card number
- Rewards/benefits may change
- Review terms before confirming

**Features:**

## Card Maintenance

### Physical Card Care

**Do:**
- Store in wallet or card holder
- Keep away from magnets and electronics
- Clean with soft cloth if needed
- Sign signature panel immediately

**Don't:**
- Bend or punch holes in card
- Expose to extreme temperatures
- Store near magnetic strips (phones, speakers)
- Laminate or apply stickers
- Share card or card details

**Chip Care:**
- Insert don't swipe when chip is present
- Keep chip clean and dry
- Don't try to remove or alter chip
- Report malfunctioning chip immediately

### Digital Wallet Management

**Supported Wallets:**
- Apple Pay
- Google Pay
- Samsung Pay
- Garmin Pay
- Fitbit Pay

**Adding Card:**
- Open wallet app
- Select "Add Card"
- Scan or enter card details
- Verify via SMS code
- Start using immediately

**Security:**
- Tokenization (actual card number not shared)
- Biometric or PIN required for payments
- Works even if physical card is lost
- Remove card from wallet if phone lost/stolen

## Troubleshooting

### Card Declined

**Common Reasons:**
- Insufficient funds/credit available
- Daily limit exceeded
- International transaction without travel notice
- Suspected fraud protection
- Card expired
- Incorrect PIN (3 attempts locks card)

**Immediate Actions:**
- Check account balance
- Verify card not expired
- Try different payment method
- Call customer service if issue persists
- May need to authorize transaction

### Card Not Working

**Chip Not Reading:**
- Insert multiple times
- Try different terminal
- Clean chip with soft cloth
- Use magnetic stripe if available
- Request replacement if problem continues

**Magnetic Stripe Issue:**
- Check for physical damage
- Try cleaning stripe
- Use chip if available
- Request replacement card

**ATM Card Captured:**
- Call number on ATM screen immediately
- Available 24/7
- Card typically held for 30 days
- May need to visit bank branch
- Free replacement issued
