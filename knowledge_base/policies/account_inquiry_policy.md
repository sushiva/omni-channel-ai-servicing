## Policy ID: POL-ACCT-001

## Overview

This policy outlines procedures for account inquiries, statement requests, balance checks, transaction history access, and document retrieval. We provide multiple convenient channels to access your account information 24/7.

## Account Balance Inquiries

### How to Check Your Balance

**Mobile App (Recommended):**
- Login → Dashboard → View balance immediately
- Available Balance: Funds you can use now
- Current Balance: Including pending transactions
- Real-time updates
- Biometric login for quick access

**Online Banking:**
- Login → Accounts → Select account → View balance
- Historical balance trends
- Download transaction reports
- Set up balance alerts

**Phone Banking:**
- Call 1-800-555-BANK (2265)
- Automated system available 24/7
- Voice authentication for quick access
- Live agent assistance during business hours

**ATM:**
- Insert card at any ATM
- Select "Balance Inquiry"
- View on screen or print receipt
- No fee at our ATMs
- Out-of-network fees may apply ($3)

**Text Banking:**
- Text "BAL" to 555-BANK (2265)
- Receive balance via SMS
- Must enroll in text banking first
- Standard text messaging rates apply
- Secure 4-digit PIN required

**In-Branch:**
- Visit any branch location
- Teller can provide balance
- Printed balance slip available
- No appointment necessary

### Understanding Your Balance

**Available Balance:**
- Funds you can spend/withdraw now
- Includes: Current balance minus pending debits and holds
- Real-time calculation
- Updates continuously
- Use this to avoid overdrafts

**Current Balance:**
- All deposits and withdrawals posted to account
- Does NOT include pending transactions
- May be higher than available balance
- Updated at end of each business day

**Pending Transactions:**
- Authorized but not yet posted
- Credit card pre-authorizations
- ATM withdrawals
- Checks not yet cleared
- ACH transfers in process

**Holds on Deposits:**
| Deposit Type | Hold Period |
|--------------|-------------|
| Cash (in-branch) | Immediate availability |
| Check from same bank | Next business day |
| Local check | 2 business days |
| Out-of-state check | 5 business days |
| Check over $5,000 | 7 business days (first $200 available next day) |
| Mobile deposit | 2 business days |

## Transaction History

### Accessing Transaction History

**Online/Mobile:**
- View last 18 months online
- Download up to 7 years via request
- Filter by date, amount, type
- Search by merchant or description
- Export to CSV, Excel, or PDF

**Transaction Details Include:**
- Date and time
- Merchant name
- Transaction amount
- Current balance after transaction
- Transaction ID (for disputes)
- Category (auto-categorized)

**Search and Filter Options:**
- By date range
- By transaction amount (min/max)
- By transaction type (debit, credit, ACH, wire)
- By merchant or description keywords
- By category (groceries, gas, dining, etc.)
- Pending vs. posted

**Export Options:**
- PDF for printing or records
- CSV for Excel or financial software
- QBO/QFX for QuickBooks
- OFX for Quicken or Mint
- Email to yourself

### Transaction Types Explained

**Debit Transactions:**
- ATM withdrawals
- Debit card purchases
- Check payments
- ACH debits (bill payments)
- Wire transfers sent
- Bank fees

**Credit Transactions:**
- Direct deposits (payroll)
- Deposits (cash, check)
- ACH credits (refunds, transfers)
- Wire transfers received
- Interest earned
- Refunds or adjustments

**Pending Transactions:**
- Authorized but not settled
- May take 1-5 business days to post
- Amount may change (gas station pre-auth)
- Can be cancelled if merchant voids quickly

**Recurring Transactions:**
- Subscriptions
- Automatic bill payments
- Scheduled transfers
- Loan payments
- Flagged with recurring icon for easy identification

## Statement Services

### Electronic Statements (eStatements)

**Benefits:**
- Available 7+ years online
- Email notification when ready
- Faster than mail (3-5 days earlier)
- Eco-friendly paperless option
- More secure (no mail theft)
- $2/month account fee waived

**Enrollment:**
- Login to online banking
- Navigate to Statements → Go Paperless
- Confirm email address
- Agree to electronic delivery terms
- Paper statements stop after enrollment

**Accessing eStatements:**
- Online banking → Statements
- Select month/year from dropdown
- View PDF in browser
- Download for your records
- Print if needed

**Features:**
- Searchable PDF format
- Bookmarks for easy navigation
- Copy/paste transaction data
- Same information as paper statement
- Compatible with all devices

### Paper Statements

**Delivery:**
- Mailed within 3-5 business days of statement closing date
- First-class mail
- Arrives 7-10 days after closing date
- Allow up to 15 days for delivery
- Report non-receipt within 30 days

**Fees:**
- Checking accounts: Free
- Savings accounts: Free
- Some account types: $2/month (waived if enrolled in eStatements)

**Statement Contents:**
- Opening and closing balance
- All transactions for statement period
- Interest earned
- Fees charged
- Year-to-date totals
- Important notices
- Routing and account numbers

**Special Requests:**
- Additional copy: $5 per statement
- Statements older than 2 years: $10 per statement
- Rush delivery: $15 (overnight)
- Certified statement: $25 (for legal purposes)

### Statement Periods

**Standard Cycles:**
- Monthly for most accounts
- Quarterly for inactive savings
- Annually for CDs and IRAs
- Custom cycles available for business accounts

**Statement Closing Dates:**
- Varies by account
- View in account details
- Usually end of month
- Can be changed (subject to restrictions)

- Instant delivery

## Special Account Inquiries

### Account Verification

**Third-Party Verification (Employer, Landlord):**
- Written authorization required
- Account existence confirmed
- Average balance provided (if authorized)
- No specific transaction details shared
- Processing: 3-5 business days
- Fee: $10

**For Court/Legal Purposes:**
- Requires subpoena or court order
- Certified statement provided
- Official bank seal affixed
- Notarized if requested
- Fee: $25-$50 depending on scope

### Historical Research

**Older Than 7 Years:**
- May be stored in archives
- Requires special request
- Extended processing time (2-4 weeks)
- Additional fees may apply ($25+)
- Not all historical records available
- Microfilm/image retrieval

**Escheatment Inquiries:**
- Dormant accounts turned over to state
- Search state unclaimed property database
- Claim form required
- Proof of ownership necessary
- We can assist with claim process

### Deceased Account Holder

**Information for Executor/Heir:**
- Death certificate required
- Letters testamentary or administration
- Proof of executor/heir status
- Account balance as of date of death
- Transaction history
- Outstanding items

**Process:**
- Visit branch or mail documentation
- Estate representative assigned
- Account access restricted until probate
- Assistance with account closure
- Transfer of funds per will/probate

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Cannot See Recent Transactions:**
- Allow 1-2 business days for posting
- Check pending transactions section
- Clear browser cache and retry
- Try mobile app instead
- Call if missing after 3 days

**Statement Not Received:**
- Check spam/junk folder (eStatements)
- Verify mailing address (paper statements)
- Allow 15 days before reporting non-receipt
- Request duplicate (may incur fee)

**Balance Doesn't Match Records:**
- Review all pending transactions
- Account for deposit holds
- Check for any returned items
- Compare to latest statement
- Contact us to reconcile

**Cannot Login to View Account:**
- Verify username and password
- Check caps lock
- Reset password if forgotten
- Clear browser cache/cookies
- Try incognito/private mode
- Call for assistance if locked out
