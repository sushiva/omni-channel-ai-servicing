### Q: What happens if my payment fails?
**A:** **We have several safeguards and options:**

**Common Reasons for Failure:**
- Insufficient funds (NSF)
- Account closed or frozen
- Payment exceeds daily limit
- Incorrect account/routing number
- Stopped payment placed

**What Happens:**
1. **Immediate Notification**: Email and app alert
2. **Automatic Retry**: We attempt next business day (for scheduled payments)
3. **NSF Fee**: $35 per failed payment (may be waived for first occurrence)
4. **Late Payment**: May be reported to payee, could incur late fees

**Your Options:**
1. **Resubmit Payment**: Manually reprocess once funds available
2. **Transfer Funds**: Move money from savings/other accounts
3. **Overdraft Protection**: Link backup account to cover shortfalls
4. **Contact Us**: Discuss fee waiver or payment plan options

**Prevention:**
- Low balance alerts (set threshold)
- Scheduled payment reminders (email/app)
- Overdraft protection
- Buffer funds in account

**Fee Waivers**: First NSF fee often waived with good account history.

### Q: Can I make a payment with cash?
**A:** **Yes, through several channels:**

**In-Branch:**
- Visit any branch location
- Bring account information
- Hand cash directly to teller
- Immediate posting, get receipt

**Cash Deposit ATM:**

- Insert bills directly (no envelope)

**Check Payment Status:**
1. Log in to online banking
2. Go to "Activity" or "Transactions"
3. Look for payment in pending or posted

**Common Issues:**

**Insufficient Funds:**
- Check account balance
- Transfer funds from savings
- Revisit after funds deposited

**Exceeded Limits:**
- Daily transfer limit reached
- Bill pay limit exceeded
- Solution: Wait until next day or request limit increase

**Incorrect Information:**
- Wrong account number
- Wrong routing number
- Expired payment method
- Solution: Update information and resubmit

**Technical Error:**
- App or website issue
- Temporary system outage
- Solution: Try again in 1-2 hours or use alternate method

**Account Restrictions:**
- Fraud alert on account
- Account frozen or closed
- Negative balance blocking
- Solution: Call customer service immediately

**Next Steps:**
- Resubmit payment once issue resolved
- Contact payee if payment was time-sensitive
- Call us if you can't identify the issue: 1-800-555-BANK

**A:** **Duplicate payment resolution:**

**If Both Payments Are Pending:**
1. Log in to online banking

**If Both Payments Posted:**

**Same-Day Duplicate:**
1. Call us immediately: 1-800-555-BANK

3. Must be reported within 2 hours of payment

**Different-Day or After Same-Day Window:**
1. **Contact Payee First**: Request refund of duplicate

   - Bill pay recipient: Refund timeline varies

3. **Monitor Account**: Watch for credit to post

**If Payee Won't Refund:**
- File dispute with us
- Provide: Both transaction records, evidence of contact with payee
- We'll investigate and potentially credit your account

**Bill Pay Duplicates:**
- Contact us: We can stop payment on check if not yet cashed
- Electronic payments: Contact us within 24 hours for possible reversal

**Prevention**: Review payment summary before confirming, set up payment confirmation alerts.

### Q: What is a returned payment fee?
**A:** **Fee charged when payment can't be processed:**

**Returned Payment (NSF) Fee:**
- **Amount**: $35 per returned payment
- **Charged When**: Insufficient funds to cover payment
- **Applies To**: Checks, ACH, scheduled payments, auto-pay

**Why It Happens:**
- Account balance too low
- Pending deposits not yet available
- Forgot about scheduled payment
- Calculation error

**Additional Consequences:**
- Payee may charge late fee
- May be reported as late payment
- Could affect credit score (for credit accounts)
- May result in service termination (utilities, subscriptions)

**Overdraft vs. NSF:**
- **Overdraft**: We cover payment, you go negative, charged overdraft fee
- **NSF**: We don't cover payment, returned to payee, charged NSF fee

**Fee Waivers:**
- First occurrence: Often waived with request
- Good account history: May waive 1-2 per year
- Financial hardship: May qualify for fee assistance program

**Prevention:**
- Low balance alerts
- Overdraft protection (link savings account)
- Account buffer (keep $100-500 minimum)
- Track spending with budgeting app

**Refund Request:** Call 1-800-555-BANK within 30 days to request fee review.
